How it Works:

  • Player 1 will always win.

This calculator shows how much elo is gained and lost from a match. The nature of the system makes the matches more meaningful if the lesser ranked player beats the higher ranked player. The current system works around the number 75 wherein a player can only lose or gain a maximum of 75 points if the match results in a majoy upset. Closer matches will get a reduced value that is relative to the number 75.
